Methodology

How we calculate savings

This calculator uses conservative multipliers based on industry benchmarks from Gartner, Forrester, and BDC's client engagements. Compliance efficiency is modeled as a percentage of governance labor cost recovered through TEMPO automation. Shadow AI risk is calculated per ungoverned tool using average incident cost data. Data quality improvement uses the standard 15-25% reduction benchmark for governed environments.

These are directional estimates, not guarantees. Your actual savings depend on organizational complexity, regulatory environment, and current governance maturity. The Calibration Call produces a more precise assessment.
