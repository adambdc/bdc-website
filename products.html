The Architecture

Four engines. Always running.

TEMPO is not a checklist. It is a system with four interdependent engines that keep governance operational, not theoretical.

Engine 1

Adopt

Structured AI adoption with governance built in from day one. Use-case evaluation, risk-tiered approval workflows, and organizational readiness. Every new AI capability enters through a governed path.

Engine 2

Govern

Operational governance infrastructure: councils, RACI matrices, decision rights, policy frameworks, and accountability structures. Not documents that sit on a shelf. Operating systems that run every day.

Engine 3

Orchestrate

Cross-functional coordination, training, role design, and change management. Governance works when the entire organization runs it, not just the compliance team. Orchestrate makes governance the operating rhythm.

Engine 4

Adapt

Continuous alignment with evolving regulations, standards, and organizational needs. TEMPO watches the regulatory horizon, maps incoming changes to your governance structure, and updates before mandates land.

The Control Layer

Three edges keep the system honest.

Edges connect the engines and enforce the operating rhythm. They are the feedback loops that prevent governance from drifting.

Throttle

Controls the pace of AI adoption. Too fast and governance cannot keep up. Too slow and shadow AI fills the gap. Throttle calibrates adoption velocity to governance capacity.

Clock

Sets the governance cadence. Review cycles, audit schedules, council meetings, reporting rhythms. Clock ensures governance operates on a regular beat, not in crisis-response mode.

Radar

Scans the regulatory and technology horizon. New standards, new risks, new capabilities. Radar feeds the Adapt engine so your governance evolves before mandates arrive.
