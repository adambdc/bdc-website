TEMPO: Adopt + Govern

Governance that travels with your AI agents.

Agentic AI changes the governance equation. When AI systems act autonomously, make decisions, and chain actions together, static policies and manual review processes break down. The governance layer has to be architectural, not procedural.

BDC designs governance frameworks that are embedded in your agentic systems from day one. Architecture patterns, risk boundaries, operational guardrails, and monitoring structures that keep pace with autonomous AI without creating bottlenecks.

This is where most organizations will need governance next. The ones that build the architecture now will be ready. The ones that wait will be retrofitting under regulatory pressure.

What you get

  • Agentic AI governance architecture design
  • Risk boundary mapping for autonomous systems
  • Operational guardrail patterns and monitoring frameworks
  • RACI matrices for human-agent decision rights
  • Board-ready agentic AI risk documentation
TEMPO: Entry Point

Know where you stand before you move.

The AI Readiness Assessment is the entry point to TEMPO's Metronome stage. It maps your ungoverned AI tools, shadow AI exposure, regulatory gaps, and organizational readiness across all four TEMPO engines.

You get a clear picture of where governance exists, where it is missing, and where the risk is compounding. The output is a prioritized action plan that connects directly to TEMPO's maturity path.

Most organizations think they know their AI exposure. The assessment consistently reveals 3 to 5x more ungoverned AI usage than leadership expects.

What you get

  • Complete AI tool and shadow AI inventory
  • Regulatory gap analysis (NIST AI RMF, state AI laws, industry-specific)
  • Governance maturity scoring across TEMPO's four engines
  • Prioritized action plan with TEMPO stage mapping
  • Executive summary with board-ready risk findings
TEMPO: Orchestrate

Turn governance from a compliance burden into an operational advantage.

AI enablement is the bridge between governance on paper and governance in practice. Training, role redesign, and change management that makes governance the way your organization works, not something layered on top.

BDC builds enablement programs that connect directly to your TEMPO implementation. Teams learn the governance framework by using it, not by reading about it. The result: faster AI adoption with governance built in from the start.

This is where the 92%/3% gap closes. Policies exist. Enablement makes them real.

What you get

  • Role-specific AI governance training programs
  • Change management strategy for governance adoption
  • Internal AI usage standards and guidelines
  • Governance champion identification and enablement
  • Adoption metrics and progress tracking against TEMPO maturity
